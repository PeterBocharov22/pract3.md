### Задача 4

(({((()))}))
{}
{()}
()
{}

```bash
BNF = '''
E = "()" | "{}" | E E | "(" E ")" | "{" E "}"
'''

for i in range(10):
    print(generate_phrase(parse_bnf(BNF), 'E'))
```
